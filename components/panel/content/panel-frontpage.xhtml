Welcome to Phoebus!

Phoebus is the software that drives Add-ons Sites targeting many UXP Applications including Pale Moon, Basilisk, and more.

What is the Panel?

The Phoebus Panel allows Add-on Developers, Add-ons Team Personnel, and Phoebus Administrators to be able to manage all the extensions and themes (and other things) contained on an Add-ons Site. You can submit and update Add-ons that supports any of the Applications that Phoebus currently serves. All you have to do is register or login.
