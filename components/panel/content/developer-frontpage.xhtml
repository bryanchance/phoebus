Welcome to Phoebus!

Phoebus is the software that drives Add-ons Sites targeting many UXP Applications including Pale Moon, Basilisk, and more.

What is the Panel?

The Phoebus Panel allows Add-on Developers, to manage their extensions and themes contained on an Add-ons Site. You can submit and update Add-ons that supports any of the Applications that Phoebus currently serves.

How do I get started?

On the banner at the top right you can select Your Add-ons to manage your extensions and themes. Additionally, you can select Your Account to alter your account settings such as your display name and password
